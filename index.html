About

A group built around extraordinary projects.

Other VC is not a fund — it is a group of people who decided to build extraordinary things together, seriously and deliberately, with a strong internal code. Most venture structures exist to allocate capital. We exist to build. We chose the name "Other" deliberately: it signals a different orientation, one that values judgment, character and long-term substance over hype cycles or financial theatre. The projects we back span AI, real estate, biotech, consumer and digital infrastructure — unified not by industry but by the quality of execution and the commitment to building things properly.
