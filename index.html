About

Not venture capital as usual.

Other VC is built around a simple conviction: breakthrough technologies deserve investors who understand both ambition and responsibility. We back companies that are structurally sound, technically serious and built to matter over time.

01 · Identity

Venturous

We look for founders willing to tackle difficult technical problems, not incremental products built for short-term noise.

02 · Ethics

Chivalry

We believe transparency, loyalty and sound judgment create stronger companies, stronger partnerships and better long-term outcomes.

03 · Position

Other

We are intentionally distinct from the logic of growth at any cost. The point is not speed alone. The point is quality, resilience and consequence.

Investment thesis

Backing the edge of possibility without losing the center of judgment.

Our thesis is designed for technically complex ventures where conviction must be paired with rigor. We invest where real science, real engineering and real market relevance intersect.

01

Deep technical substance

We favor work that has defensibility, complexity and a genuine barrier to entry.

02

Founder discipline

We value founders who can balance ambition with clarity, focus and accountability.

03

Long-horizon company building

We are interested in durable systems, not just short-cycle momentum.

How we think

Capital is only one layer of support.

The best relationships are built on alignment, not transaction. We aim to bring useful perspective to the founders we back: strategic clarity, technical respect and steady judgment when the work becomes difficult.

Focus areas

Where we look hardest.

We focus on sectors where technical leverage can redefine entire categories. The common denominator is not trendiness. It is depth, consequence and real potential.

Biotechnology

Platforms, tools and breakthroughs capable of reshaping how health, biology and applied science move forward.

  • Life sciences infrastructure
  • Applied biology platforms
  • Research-driven therapeutics

Artificial Intelligence

Systems that turn computation into practical leverage, particularly where intelligence compounds operational advantage.

  • AI-native infrastructure
  • Domain-specific intelligence
  • Tools that scale decision quality

Deep Tech

Foundational technologies with long build cycles, strong defensibility and category-level impact.

  • Frontier engineering
  • Novel technical architecture
  • Long-horizon industrial innovation
