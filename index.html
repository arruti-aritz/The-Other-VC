About

A group built around extraordinary projects.

Other VC brings together people who want to build ambitious things properly. The emphasis is not on looking like a conventional VC, but on creating meaningful projects with conviction, discipline and long-term intent.

"Other" signals a different point of view: one that values judgment, character and enduring substance over hype or financial theatre.
