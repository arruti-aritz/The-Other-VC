Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

Other VC is a group of people building extraordinary projects across technology, real estate, biotech and consumer sectors. This website is operated by Other VC and can be reached at contact@othervc.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate this website:

  • Contact data. If you write to us at contact@othervc.com, we receive your email address and any information you include in your message.
  • Usage data. We may collect anonymised technical data such as browser type, pages visited and time spent on site through standard server logs or analytics tools.

We do not operate registration, user accounts or payment processing on this website.

3. How we use your data

Contact data is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to mailing lists, sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

This website uses the following third-party services which may process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — used to store and serve news content published on this site. No personal data is stored in Supabase by this website.
  • Google Fonts — used to load typefaces. Google may collect your IP address when fonts are requested. See Google's privacy policy for details.
  • GitHub Pages — used to host this website. GitHub may collect standard server log data.

5. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. A session cookie may be stored temporarily if you access the admin section. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

6. Your rights

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have rights under the GDPR including the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@othervc.com.

7. Data retention

We retain email correspondence for as long as it is relevant to the enquiry, and no longer than 24 months.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.

9. Contact

For any questions about this policy, write to contact@othervc.com.
