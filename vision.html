Vision

A group for building extraordinary projects properly.

Other VC is not a venture capital firm. It is a group of people who want to build ambitious projects with seriousness, character and long-term intent.

Position

Not a conventional VC.

Other VC is not organized around the standard language or operating logic of venture capital. The point is not to act like a financial wrapper around projects. The point is to help create extraordinary work with stronger judgment, deeper commitment and a better internal standard.

This means being selective, serious and involved. It means caring about what is being built, how it is being built and whether it deserves to endure.

Venturous

Move toward the difficult.

We are interested in projects with technical depth, structural ambition and the potential to matter. The group is drawn toward work that requires courage and real effort.

Chivalry

Build with an internal code.

We believe loyalty, transparency, seriousness and responsibility are part of strong building. They are not decorative values. They shape how the work gets done.

Why “Other” matters

“Other” signals a difference in orientation. It means the group does not want to reproduce the default venture narrative. It wants to create something more grounded, more deliberate and more credible.

Why the work is broad

The ventures span AI, consumer, real estate, digital systems and biotech because the group is not defined by one industry. It is defined by the quality and seriousness of the work.

What the vision asks for

It asks for conviction without noise, ambition without emptiness and execution without losing ethical seriousness. That is the standard the group is trying to hold.
