Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owner.

2. How we use cookies

This website uses a minimal number of cookies and does not use any advertising or tracking cookies.

3. Cookies we use

Session cookies (strictly necessary)

If you access the admin section of this website, a session cookie named ovc_admin_auth is stored in your browser's sessionStorage. This cookie exists only for the duration of your browser session and is deleted automatically when you close the tab. It does not track your activity or transmit data to any server.

Third-party cookies

Google Fonts may set cookies or store data when font files are loaded from Google's servers. We do not control these cookies. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies we do not use

We do not use:

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Any cookies that track your behaviour across other websites

5. How to control cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies entirely or delete existing ones. Note that disabling cookies will not affect the functionality of this website for standard visitors.

For guidance on managing cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if our use of cookies changes. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.

7. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at contact@othervc.com.
