Portfolio

Projects in progress.

A set of ventures being built across AI, digital infrastructure, consumer products, real estate and biotech.

Overview

Distinct projects, different sectors, shared standards.

The portfolio is organized by area, but what ties it together is not industry. It is the shared desire to build ambitious things properly, with seriousness, coherence and long-term intent.

AI & Tech

Growbyte

The core brand for tech-driven innovation, digital systems and scalable infrastructure.

Growbyte is the central vehicle for a broad range of digital projects and experiments. It includes ATS systems, custom websites and digital tools.

Under Growbyte sits webovka, focused on websites and digital products. It represents the most execution-oriented side of the group’s work.

AI Product

Instadeck

A next-generation AI presentation generator.

Instadeck is built to streamline how people create decks by combining automated design, content structuring and a more intelligent workflow for presentation building.

The goal is not only speed, but a more coherent and polished final result.

AI Consultancy

Rainoc

An AI agentic consultancy for implementing custom AI agents into companies.

Rainoc focuses on building web-based systems where orchestrated LLMs handle specific parts of business logic in real organizations.

It is centered on useful implementation rather than generic AI hype.

Consumer & Lifestyle

Ocurca

A healthy lifestyle brand starting as a community and expanding into premium products.

Ocurca begins with an Instagram-first audience strategy, building identity and community around healthier living and premium taste.

The roadmap then moves into white-label products such as olive oil or sardines, and eventually into physical locations.

Real Estate

EST8

A real estate project beginning with a family reconstruction and expanding into proptech.

EST8 starts by reconstructing an old family house into flats, turning a real asset into the foundation of a broader direction.

Over time, the vision expands into a tech-driven agency, an AI mortgage helper and eventually a crowdfunding layer.

Biotech

Darwic

A biotech startup using protein large language models to engineer enzymes for industrial use.

Darwic applies pLLMs to modify DNA sequences of enzymes, with the aim of making them withstand extreme temperatures in industrial environments.

It represents the most science-intensive side of the group’s work.
